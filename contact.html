My Favorite Team

If you could not tell by now, my favorite team is FC Barcelona. I was born into this fandom since my family supports this club. Some consider them to be one of the most prolific clubs in the world. They have won many trophies in their history, have had some of the best players in history, and are more often than not in title contention for all the leagues and competitions they are in. Lionel Messi is my favorite player since he is, in my opinion, the best player to have played the game. Barcelona's play style is unique and I grew up loving their style of play. They will always be the club I support proudly.
